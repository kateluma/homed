Follow a recipe

  1. Make as much use of all food as posible,do not waste it during preparation
  2. Store food properly
  3. Turn down the burner as soon as the food reaches boiling point
  4. Have the food ready to put in the oven as soon as the oven is hot
  5. Most ovens are well insulated and hold an oven temperature for a short time after they have been turned off
